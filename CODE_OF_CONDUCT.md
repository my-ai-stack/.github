# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our projects a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to [admin@stack-ai.me](mailto:admin@stack-ai.me).

**Version 2.1, adopted from [contributor-covenant.org](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/)**
